School Life

School Hall

The Largest Indoor Area of The School

The School Hall can be used for many things e.g. assemblies, PE lessons(when it's raining outside), special events like Christmas Dinner and ceremonies like the End of Year Awards.



School Library

Has to Be Re-organized every 2 Weeks

The School Library has so many people going to it every day that it has to be re-organized so often. There is no returning policy exceot that you have to have returned all the books you borrowed by the end of the academic year.



School Playground

Largest Outdoor Facility in The School

The School Playground has a very big field that we can play sports on which oversees the freeway. On the concrete, there are markings on the floor that mark out games such as Snakes and Ladders, Chess, What's the Time Mr Wolf and more. There is also a nature area and a little obstacle course.
